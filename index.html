I'm a frontend developer from Portugal, currently building my skills while finishing a technical junior programme at ETPR (Escola Técnica e Profissional do Ribatejo). I care about interfaces that feel considered — clean UI, deliberate motion, nothing left to default.

Off the frontend, I run the infrastructure behind Craft and Helps, a Minecraft MMORPG community — from the game server itself to the Azuriom-powered web platform sitting on top of it. Rack management, reverse proxies and query optimization are second nature at this point.

Outside of code: I'm into 16-bit pixel art and video editing, and I'm generally curious about whatever new technology crosses my feed.
